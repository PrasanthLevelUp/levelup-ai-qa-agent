# COMPREHENSIVE TEST DATA DEMO — Sprint 3

All intents below are from ONE requirement (User Registration with username max 20 chars).

---

## 1. POSITIVE (realistic, executable values)

Title: "Create a record with valid data"
Coverage: positive

Steps:
- Open the page under test
- Enter "Sample Username" in the Username field
- Enter "emma.smith@example.com" in the Email field
- Enter "9876543210" in the Phone field
- Enter "34" in the Age field
- Enter "Sample Notes" in the Notes field    ← UNKNOWN FIELD fallback: "Sample <Label>"
- Click the Register button

## 2. BOUNDARY (username 20-char limit grounded in requirement)
Title: "The username must not exceed 20 characters"
Coverage: boundary
Steps (first 3):
- Open the page under test
- Enter a Username of 21 characters (1 over the 20-character maximum) in the Username field
- Enter a Email of 21 characters (1 over the 20-character maximum) in the Email field
- Enter a Phone of 21 characters (1 over the 20-character maximum) in the Phone field

## 3. DUPLICATE
Title: "Duplicate / unique-constraint violation is handled"
- Open the page under test
- Enter a Username that already exists (a duplicate of an existing record) in the Username field
- Enter a Email that already exists (a duplicate of an existing record) in the Email field
- Enter a Phone that already exists (a duplicate of an existing record) in the Phone field

## 4. SQL INJECTION
Title: "SQL-injection input is rejected safely"
- Open the page under test
- Enter the SQL-injection string "' OR 1=1 --" in the Username field
- Enter the SQL-injection string "' OR 1=1 --" in the Email field
- Enter the SQL-injection string "' OR 1=1 --" in the Phone field

## 5. XSS
Title: "XSS / script payload is escaped, not executed"
- Open the page under test
- Enter the XSS payload "<script>alert(1)</script>" in the Username field
- Enter the XSS payload "<script>alert(1)</script>" in the Email field
- Enter the XSS payload "<script>alert(1)</script>" in the Phone field

## 6. WHITESPACE
Title: "Whitespace-only required fields are rejected"
- Open the page under test
- Enter a whitespace-only Username (e.g. "   ") in the Username field
- Enter a whitespace-only Email (e.g. "   ") in the Email field
- Enter a whitespace-only Phone (e.g. "   ") in the Phone field

## 7. UNICODE
Title: "Non-ASCII or special characters in the username are rejected"
- Open the page under test
- Enter a unicode Username (e.g. "தமிழ்", "李雷", "😊") in the Username field
- Enter a unicode Email (e.g. "தமிழ்", "李雷", "😊") in the Email field
- Enter a unicode Phone (e.g. "தமிழ்", "李雷", "😊") in the Phone field
